Community Node

The Community Node is a collective of Hamonauts, who will actively participate in the ecosystem to ensure that we do our part as a community, to ensure the success of the Harmony Poject. We recognize that the problems with centralization cannot be fixed with code alone. It requires a community. Working together, we can make a change. If you would like to be a part of the movement, feel free to reach out and join us. We know that Harmony is young, but we seek to actively participate in chain governance as the Harmony Team looks to the community for decentralization
